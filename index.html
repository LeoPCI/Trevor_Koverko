About Me.

I am a technology entrepreneur currently located in Toronto. In 2015 my Oculus Rift based project became the world's first VR exit. Prior to that I founded eProf.com

Aside from launching companies, I like to invest in both boring cash flow businesses and 'moonshot' ideas. In 2016 I was one of the most active angel investors in Canada with early positions in ShapeShift.io, Luminex and Royalty Exchange. These days I run a private equity firm that acquires profitable web businesses. I also am a leading seed investor in the bitcoin/blockchain ecosystem since 2012. I seriously love blockchain technology and I advise some of the world's leading decentralized projects.

I was an original advisor/investor to the Ethereum project. Other notable blockchain team I've seeded include: Factom, MaidSafe and Tether. I'm always looking for new interesting blockchain projects to be involved in, especially Initial Coin Offerings.

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you are too.
